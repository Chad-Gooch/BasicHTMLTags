Another anime that takes place in another world

Obviously there are a ton of anime that take place in another world.

Common threads in otherworld anime.

  • New world has magic.
  • You are more powerful in the new world.
  • You get your own harem.
  • You don't have to have a day job.
  • You get to keep your memories from the old world
  • The new world is less technologically advanced.

So what makes this world different?

  • The main character isn't an awkward teenager.
  • The main character isn't the strongest one in the series.
  • Excellent world-building.
  • Character building other than the main character.
  • Main character is aware of his impact on the world.
